Oak House

The objective of the Oak House project is to design a modern luxury home that combines sleek, contemporary aesthetics with timeless materials. The focal point of the design will be the double-height living area, creating a dramatic and expansive space that emphasizes openness and sophistication. The use of wood and travertine, both durable and luxurious materials, will be key to balancing warmth and elegance with a modern, clean aesthetic. The goal is to create an environment that exudes luxury, comfort, and style, with a focus on high-end finishes, seamless design, and exceptional craftsmanship..

The Oak House stands as a tribute to modern elegance, featuring travertine stone and a clean architectural style. Crafted to captivate with its timeless beauty and practical design, embodying sophistication at its finest. A true statement of contemporary luxury for the discerning homeowner.

Challenges

Balancing a rustic aesthetic with modern conveniences and functionality. Sourcing high-quality materials that align with the project's design ethos. Creating an open-plan space that feels cozy and connected to nature.

Solutions

The use of natural stone and timber complements the rustic charm while maintaining a contemporary edge. Locally sourced materials were prioritized to enhance authenticity and sustainability.
